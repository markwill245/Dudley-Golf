Our Story

Designed by Greatness • Est. 1893

In 1893, Canon Adam Gray Maitland arrived from Scotland to find a void in the Black Country: the absence of the "Royal and Ancient game." He set his sights on 'The Old Park' and called upon a legend.

Old Tom Morris—the architect of Muirfield, Prestwick, and Carnoustie—personally designed the original course, establishing a standard of excellence that remains today.

The Ascent

Located on the Rough Hills, our course is built on volcanic rock covered in thousands of years of soft, fine grass.

The Vista

From the 8th green, look East. On a clear day, there is nothing higher until you reach the Ural Mountains in Russia.
